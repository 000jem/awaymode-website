What it is

A focus timer with a real shield behind it.

Most focus apps run a timer and hope you don't open Twitter. Holt uses Apple's Screen Time framework to actually hide the apps you nominated for the length of the session. The shield holds until the timer ends; in strict mode, it holds even if you change your mind.

01 / Blocking

Screen Time, not a fake overlay.

Holt uses Family Controls to shield apps at the OS level. Tap a blocked app during a session and iOS hands back a system shield, not a Holt modal. The same protection Apple ships in Screen Time, scoped to your session.

02 / Privacy

Zero accounts, zero analytics, zero servers.

Session history, mode configurations, and your Screen Time selection live in SwiftData on the device. The App Store privacy nutrition label declares Data Not Collected. Uninstalling the app removes everything.

03 / Native

Built for iOS, not ported.

Live Activities on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. Lock Screen widgets. Mindful Minutes in Apple Health, if you opt in. Shortcuts and App Intents for Siri. Force-quit recovery — kill the app mid-session and it picks back up where you were.

04 / Pomodoro

The chain math is correct.

A 25 × 4 Pomodoro is 115 minutes wall-clock with breaks, not 100. Holt notifies you at every phase boundary, logs work blocks only to history, and re-schedules the chain correctly if you pause or the app gets killed mid-cycle.

Modes

Four shapes of sit.

Three built-in modes covering the most common commitments, plus a fourth for anything else. Each is one tap from the home screen.

Quick
10 min

One block. For the in-between moments — a hard email, an inbox sweep, a single design pass.

Pomodoro
25 × 4 + 5m

Classic Cirillo chain. 115 minutes wall-clock with three breaks. Phase notifications at every transition.

Deep
90 min · strict

Strict mode. The shield holds for the entire session — you cannot end it early. For the day the work has to land.

Custom
your call

Pick the minutes, the apps, the cycles, and the break length. Save as many as you need.

What it doesn't do

A few things on purpose.

Worth knowing before you download, so the absence isn't a surprise.

No streaks Nothing dies if you skip a day. Consistency is its own reward; we don't need to threaten you about it.
No social features No leaderboards, no shared trees, no friend invites. Focus is a solo act.
No cloud sync (v1) Your history lives on this device. iCloud sync is on the roadmap; v1 keeps the surface small.
No iPad or Mac (v1) iPhone only at launch. iPad is a likely v1.1 if there's demand.
No ads, ever Holt is paid software or it isn't anything. No ad SDK will ever live in this binary.
No AI coach No suggestions about what you should be focusing on. The mode is the mode; the work is the work.
