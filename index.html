Coming to the App Store

Holt is a focus app for iOS. You pick a length of time, choose what to shut out, and sit. The apps you flagged disappear. The room goes quiet. When the time is up, Fen lets you know.

No accounts. No analytics. No tree to plant or coach to please. Everything stays on your phone.

Quiet by design

No streaks dying, no shame loops, no celebratory popups. Just a session, then the next. Progress is felt, not announced.

Real app blocking

Uses Apple's Screen Time. The apps you pick are gone for the length of the sit — not greyed out, gone. Strict mode holds the latch.

Private by default

No servers, no telemetry. Your session history lives in SwiftData on your device. Apple's privacy nutrition label says it all.

Built for iOS

Live Activities, Lock Screen widgets, Mindful Minutes, Shortcuts. The full native stack. No web wrapper.

What you get

  • Quick — a single 10-minute sit, for the in-between moments.
  • Pomodoro — the classic 25/5 chain, four times through.
  • Deep — 90 minutes, strict. The latch stays shut.
  • Custom — make your own. Pick the minutes, pick the apps.

About Fen

Fen is a fennec fox. He's calm, capable, and in his prime. He doesn't cheer or shame. He keeps watch while you work, and notices when you bail. That's all.
