How it works

three taps from open to gone.

The point of awaymode is that you stop thinking about it. Set up is short, the latch is hard to break, and the rest of the time the app is quiet.

01

Pick what to block.

iOS Screen Time lets you flag apps or whole categories (Social, Games, News). Each awaymode mode keeps its own list.

02

Pick how long.

Flow, Focus, Deep, or your own custom mode. Strict modes don't have an end-early button. By design.

03

Lock in.

iOS hides what you flagged for the duration. The Live Activity sits on your Lock Screen. When the timer ends, your apps come back.
