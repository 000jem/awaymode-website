Privacy Policy

Last updated November 14, 2026

Summary

Holt is designed so that we never have access to your data. The app runs entirely on your iPhone. We do not operate any server that receives your activity, identity, or content. There are no user accounts. There is no analytics SDK. There is no tracking.

The App Store privacy nutrition label for Holt declares Data Not Collected. This policy explains what that means in practice and where the small amount of on-device data lives.

Data stored on your device

Holt persists the following information locally, using Apple's SwiftData framework. None of it is transmitted off your device by Holt.

  • Session history. The start time, planned length, actual length, completion status, and mode of each focus session.
  • Focus modes. The custom modes you create — names, durations, cycle counts, break lengths, and the iOS Screen Time selection associated with each.
  • App preferences. Settings such as default mode, theme, notification toggles, and onboarding completion.
  • Screen Time tokens. When you select apps, categories, or websites to block, iOS returns opaque activity tokens. Holt stores these tokens to re-apply your selection at the start of each session. Tokens cannot be used to read app names, look up apps, or identify the device. They only function on the device that issued them.

All of the above is removed if you delete Holt from your iPhone. There is no Holt server-side copy because there is no Holt server.

Data we do not collect

Holt does not collect, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials.
  • Device identifiers, advertising identifiers, or any IDFA.
  • Usage analytics, telemetry, crash reports containing user content, or session metrics.
  • Your location, IP address, contacts, calendar, photos, microphone, or camera.
  • The list of apps installed on your device.
  • The contents of any app you have asked Holt to block.

Third parties

Holt does not embed any third-party analytics, advertising, attribution, or A/B testing SDKs. The only Apple frameworks Holt interacts with are:

  • FamilyControls / ManagedSettings / DeviceActivity — Apple's Screen Time framework, used to apply the app shield. Selection tokens are stored on-device and never leave it.
  • StoreKit 2 — Apple's in-app purchase framework, used for the subscription and lifetime purchase. Apple processes payment; Holt receives only an on-device receipt.
  • HealthKit — only if you grant access. See Apple Health below.
  • UserNotifications — for the session-end and phase-transition alerts. Notifications are scheduled locally and fired by iOS; no server is involved.
  • WidgetKit, ActivityKit, AppIntents — for widgets, Live Activities, and Shortcuts. These run entirely on-device.

Apple Health

If you opt in via Settings, Holt writes completed focus sessions to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes. This integration is one-way: Holt writes; Holt does not read. The permission is requested through Apple's standard system dialog and can be revoked at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → Holt. Revoking the permission has no effect on Holt's core functionality.

Purchases and subscriptions

Holt offers optional in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit. When you make a purchase, Apple handles payment processing and returns an opaque receipt to your device confirming what you bought. Holt reads this receipt on-device to unlock paid features. The receipt does not contain your name, email, payment information, or any other personal data.

Subscription cancellation, refunds, and billing inquiries are managed through your Apple ID. Holt has no access to your payment details and cannot process refunds. For refunds, visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

Children

Holt is rated 4+ on the App Store. The app collects no personal information from any user, including children under 13. Holt is compliant with COPPA and the GDPR's requirements regarding children's data because there is no children's data — or any data — being collected.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy in a way that affects how data is handled, the change will be reflected on this page with a new last updated date, and a brief summary will appear in the app's release notes for the next version. Because there is no email list, there is no out-of-band channel through which we could notify you.

Contact

Questions about this policy or about Holt's privacy practices: hello@holt.app. Replies come from a person, not an automation.
