Holt

Privacy

Holt collects nothing. There is nothing for us to lose because there is nothing for us to hold.

The short version. Holt has no servers. We don't have an account system. We don't run analytics. We don't ship usage data to anyone. Everything the app remembers about you lives only on your phone, and uninstalling Holt removes it.

What stays on your device

Holt keeps a small amount of data locally so the app can do its job. None of it ever leaves your phone unless you explicitly choose to share it via iOS.

  • Session history. The length of each sit, whether it completed or you stepped away, and the mode you used. Stored in Apple's on-device SwiftData store.
  • Your modes and preferences. The custom modes you've made, which apps you've selected to block, and your settings choices.
  • Screen Time selection. When you pick apps to block, Apple gives Holt an opaque token that refers to those apps. We never see the app names. The token only works on your device.

What we don't collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account.
  • No analytics. No usage metrics. No crash reports that contain your data.
  • No advertising identifiers. No tracking across apps or websites.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts, calendar, photos, or microphone access.

Apple Health

If you opt in, Holt will write completed focus sessions to Apple Health as Mindful Minutes. This is one-way: Holt writes to Health, Holt does not read from Health. You can revoke this in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health at any time, and Holt will keep working without it.

Apple's role

If you download Holt from the App Store or subscribe through it, Apple handles that transaction. Apple's own privacy policy covers that interaction, not us. We never see your payment details.

Subscriptions and purchases

Holt may offer in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit. Apple processes the payment and returns an opaque receipt to the device confirming what you bought. Holt reads this receipt on your phone to unlock paid modes. The receipt does not contain your name, email, or payment information.

Children

Holt is rated 4+. It collects no personal data, so it's safe for any age. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes in a way that affects how your data is handled, the change will be noted here and in the next app update's release notes. Because we don't have an email list, there's no other way we can reach you, and we like it that way.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@holt.app. We'll respond from a real address, not an automation.
